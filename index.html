History

The bot was first launched in 2017 following @pmarca's departure from Twitter. A few people noticed that Marc continued liking tweets, so the idea was born to make a bot that will highlight all the tweets he liked. The code was developed in my spare time and launched on a Raspberry Pi from my apartment:
